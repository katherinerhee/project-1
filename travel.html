Travel Journals

During the Summer of 2010, I spent a month studying abroad in Paris and was also able to take trips on the weekends to London, England and Dublin, Ireland. I hope you enjoy reading about my adventures in Europe and that it will inspire you to consider studying abroad too!

I've split my journals into weeks in Paris so that they are more manageable and so that you don't get a headache reading one long page about a month in Paris. I also created separate journals for my trips to Dublin and London.

Paris - Week 1:

Paris - Week 1: Days 1-7 of Summer 2010 Study Abroad

Eiffel Tower, Ile de Cite, Notre Dame, Sainte Chappelle, Modern Opera House, Canal St. Martin, Sainte Eustache, Hotel de Ville, Sacre Coeur, Moulin Rouge, and Versailles.

Paris - Week 2:

Paris - Week 2: Days 8-13 of Summer 2010 Study Abroad

Flea and Antique Markets, La Refuge des Fondus, Louvre, Hotel des Invalides (Napoleon's tomb), Paris Money Museum and Stock Exchange, and Musee d'Orsay.

Photo caption: Paris - Week 2: Louvre

London:

London: Days 13-15 of Summer 2010 Study Abroad

St. Pancras and Kings Cross Station, Trafalgar Square, Changing of the Guards at Buckingham Palace, St. James Park, Buckingham Palace, Westminster Abbey, Big Ben and Parliament, London Eye, Tower of London, Tower Bridge, St. Paul Cathedral, Shakespeare's Globe Theater, Sherlock Holmes Museum, Queen Mary's Gardens, Harry Potter's Platform 9 3/4's

Photo caption: London - Changing of the Guards

Paris - Week 3:

Paris - Week 3: Days 16-20 of Summer 2010 Study Abroad

Catacombs, Jardin de Luxembourg, Statue of Liberty in Luxembourg Gardens, UNESCO, Notre Dame, Pere Lachaise, and Musee Marmottan.

Photo caption: Paris - Week 3: Luxembourg Gardens

Dublin: Days 21-23 of Summer 2010 Study Abroad

Dollymount Beach, Trinity College, Dame Street, Christ Church Cathedral, Saint Auddens, Brazen Head Pub, Four Courts, Gallagher's Boxty House, Dublin Post Office, Dublin Spire, Georgian Doors, Custom House, Samuel Beckett Bridge, Dublin Castle, Saint Patrick's Cathedral, Guinness Brewery, Stephen's Green Park, and Grafton Street.
